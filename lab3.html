Stock Market Basics

The stock market allows investors to buy ownership in companies. It operates based on supply and demand, with stock prices fluctuating based on investor sentiment, earnings reports, and market conditions.

  • Stock exchanges include the NYSE and NASDAQ.
  • Investors can buy stocks, bonds, and ETFs.
  • Market trends are influenced by economic indicators.
