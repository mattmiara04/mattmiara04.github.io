Essential Workout Principles

  • Consistency is key - aim for at least 3 workouts per week.
  • Focus on proper form to prevent injuries and maximize results.
  • Combine strength training with cardio for balanced fitness.
  • Gradually increase weight or resistance to continue progressing.
  • Allow adequate rest between workouts for muscle recovery.
  • Stay hydrated and maintain proper nutrition to fuel your workouts.
